# Deal Folder Structure

| Ordner | Zweck | Owner | Vertraulichkeit | Namensregel |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 00_Admin | Mandat, Konflikt, GwG, Engagement, Team | Partner / Matter Manager | Privileged | YYYY-MM-DD_Typ_Version |
| 01_Intake | Anfrage, Teaser, NDA, Term Sheet | Associate | Privileged | Quelle_Datum_Betreff |
| 02_Datenraum | Datenraumspiegel, Index, Uploads | DD Lead | je nach Dokument | VDR-ID_Dokumenttitel_Version |
| 03_DD | Findings, Review Grids, Reports | Workstream Leads | Privileged | Workstream_Dokument_Status |
| 04_QA | IRL, Q&A, Follow-ups | Q&A Manager | Privileged | QA-ID_Thema_Status |
| 05_Structure | Struktur, Umwandlung, Tax, Funds Flow | Corporate / Tax | Privileged | Struktur_Stand_Datum |
| 06_Documents | NDA, SPA, APA, Disclosure, Ancillaries | Drafting Lead | Privileged | Dokument_Runde_Partei |
| 07_Signing_Closing | CP, Deliverables, Vollmachten, Signaturen | Closing Manager | Privileged | CP-ID_Deliverable_Status |
| 08_Regulatory | Fusionskontrolle, FDI, MAR, WpÜG | Regulatory Lead | Clean Room möglich | Jurisdiktion_Thema_Status |
| 09_PMI | Integration, Synergien, Day One | PMI Lead | Normal / Privileged | PMI_Workstream_Datum |
| 90_Archive | Closing Bible, Audit Trail, Final Versions | Closing Manager | Privileged | Final_Dokument_Datum |

## Regeln

- Keine Clean-Room-Datei in Normalordner kopieren.
- Jede finale Datei braucht Quelle, Version, Owner und Freigabe.
- Versandpakete erhalten eine eigene Prüfliste vor Versand.
